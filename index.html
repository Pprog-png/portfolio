Edition 01 — Visual Design & Storytelling
Fig. 1 — The Designer

Hello, I'm Prayag Ghosh. Multidisciplinary Visual Designer & Storyteller. I create high-impact digital experiences driven by intentionality, structure, and emotion. For me, design is about more than just aesthetics — it's about crafting a cohesive narrative, whether that's through a sleek publication layout, a bold brand identity, or cinematic visuals. I obsess over the details that make a project memorable: the perfect typographic pairing, the exact balance of a grid, and the precise color grading that captures a specific mood.

What I Do
01. Graphic & Layout Design Translating complex information into beautiful, scannable print and digital layouts — from multi-page editorial magazines (like Samikhya and KSAC Kaptures) to high-impact promotional posters.
02. Branding & Identity Developing cohesive visual languages from scratch, with a strong appreciation for bold typography and striking, retro-inspired aesthetics.
03. Visual Storytelling Combining digital photography, creative direction, and cinematic poster design to build immersive worlds for events and media.
